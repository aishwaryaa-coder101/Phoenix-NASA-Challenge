MEET THE TEAM

We are the Phoenix team- Aishwaryaa Premsudha, Shanel Sania and Vaaruni Saxena. We'd describe ourselves as individuals who strive towards a greater aspect that focuses on combining Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts and Mathematics. We have chosen the “Turning STEM into STEAM Challenge”. From our childhood, we have been extremely interested in the field of Science and Space. We are avid readers and researchers of the secrets and mysteries that lie in the endless celestial body that we call space. But we are sure that it is the right time to share our passion with others as well. That is why we chose this challenge to inculcate the fascination for space in young leaders.
