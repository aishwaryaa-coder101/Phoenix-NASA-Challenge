WHAT IS THE CHALLENGE?

We have chosen the NASA Project EXO-MARS. EXO-Mars' main mission was to find sources of civilizations and analyse the habitable conditions of Mars. Unfortunately there were many hastles and problems during the expedition:-
1- CRASH OF THE SCHIAPARELLI LANDER ON MARS DUE TO PREMATURE RELEASE OF PARACHUTE
2- THE GNC SENSORS LOST COLLECTION WITH THE BASE FOR JUST ONE MINUTE WHICH CAUSED THE FIRST COURSE TO FAIL PARTIALLY.
3- THE RUSSIAN-UKRAINE WAR WHICH CAUSED A LOT OF FINANCIAL AND ECONOMIC LOSS. DUE TO THIS, THE EUROPEAN SPACE AGENCY IS PLANNING TO PARTNER WITH OTHER CORPORATIONS BUT THAT WOULD REQUIRE THE ENTIRE PROCESS TO START (MANY FUNDS LOST)
